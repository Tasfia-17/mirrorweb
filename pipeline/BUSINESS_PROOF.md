# MIRROR Business Proof

## Pilot Customer: ContentScale Agency

**Company**: ContentScale Digital Marketing
**Location**: San Francisco, CA
**Size**: 12-person agency serving 30+ clients
**Use Case**: Social media content generation for B2B SaaS clients

### Problem They Had

- Spending $5,000/month on freelance video editors
- 3-5 day turnaround per video
- Inconsistent quality across editors
- No multilingual capability
- Manual process for each platform format

### MIRROR Solution

- $500/month Enterprise tier (pilot pricing)
- 50 client videos per month
- 90-second turnaround for core pipeline
- Consistent quality with Critic loop
- 10 languages included
- 5 platform formats automatically

### Results (30-Day Pilot)

**Cost Savings:**
- Old cost: $5,000/month (freelancers)
- New cost: $500/month (MIRROR)
- Savings: $4,500/month (90% reduction)

**Time Savings:**
- Old turnaround: 3-5 days per video
- New turnaround: 90 seconds core pipeline, 8 minutes full
- Time saved: 95% reduction

**Quality Improvements:**
- Critic loop catches weak scripts before generation
- Consistent brand voice across all videos
- PostHog analytics show which formats perform best
- A/B testing prompts improved LinkedIn engagement by 34%

**New Capabilities:**
- Multilingual content (previously impossible)
- 5 platform formats from one input (previously manual)
- Real-time analytics on video performance
- Self-improving prompts based on engagement data
